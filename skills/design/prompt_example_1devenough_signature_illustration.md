## ğŸ‡ºğŸ‡¸ ì˜ì–´ í”„ë¡¬í”„íŠ¸ (English Prompt Version)

Stylish Corporate Memphis style flat vector illustration preferred by big tech companies.

[Character Style Rules]
Human characters must have slightly small heads (micro-head), sturdy bodies, and slightly large hands and feet.

[Scene Description]
A conceptual diagram showing a team workflow arranged in a diamond shape.
- Center Bottom: Human developer sitting at a desk, typing.
- Center Top: Planning Robot with a thought bubble.
- Far Left: Architect Robot holding a blueprint.
- Far Right: Coding Robot using a laptop.
- Simple arrows connect them in a cycle.

[Art Style]
- Lineless (No Outlines). No Strokes. Shapes are defined by color blocks only.

[Scene & Props]
- Arrows: Express a cyclical flow.

[Character Details (Keep)]
- Human:
    - Head: Micro-head.
    - Face: Light skin tone, minimalist features (dots for eyes).
    - Hair: Short, neat black hair (simple solid shape).
    - Outfit: Yellow hoodie, dark pants.
- Robots: Planning (Top), Architect (Left), Coding (Right). Simple geometric forms.

[Color & Shading]
- Saturation: Use muted, pastel, and soft tones.
- Brightness: Low saturation but not dark. Ensure Value (V) in HSV is at least 30.
- Shading: Solid blocks of darker color for shadows. Low contrast shading (shadows should not be too black, just a slightly darker shade of the base color).
- Background color is Mint.
